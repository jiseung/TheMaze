You've lost your way along these winding paths,
You cannot see the light from where you came.
It's seeming bleak, you cannot do the math...
You look for something -- hope? Redemption? Fame?
You know not what your real objective is,
You realize with sudden 'piphany.
For no one ever told you what was this,
A maze? A game? A way to bring you glee?
These walls seem dark and unfamiliar,
For are they real or simply in your mind?
This feeling isn't new, but quite bizarre,
The knowing, understanding of this kind.
This maze may seem too long, but know the scheme--
Virtually, life is always but a dream.
